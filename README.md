# Sales Performance Analysis of Walmart Stores Using Advanced MySQL Techniques

Project Overview:
Walmart, a major retail chain, operates across several cities, offering a wide range of products. The dataset
contains detailed transaction data, including customer demographics, product lines, sales figures, and
payment methods. This project consist of using advanced SQL techniques to uncover actionable insights into sales
performance, customer behavior, and operational efficiencies.

Identifying the Top Branch by Sales Growth Rate
identified which branch has exhibited the highest sales growth over time. Analyzed the total sales
for each branch and compare the growth rate across months to find the top performer.

Finding the Most Profitable Product Line for Each Branch
determined which product line contributes the highest profit to each branch.calculated The profit margin based on the difference between the gross income and cost of goods sold.

Analyzing Customer Segmentation Based on Spending
segmented customers based on their average spending behavior. Classified customers into three
tiers: High, Medium, and Low spenders based on their total purchase amounts.

Detecting Anomalies in Sales Transactions
Detected that some transactions have unusually high or low sales compared to the average for the
product line. suspected and identified these anomalies.

Most Popular Payment Method by City
determined the most popular payment method in each city to tailor marketing strategies.

Monthly Sales Distribution by Gender
understanding the sales distribution between male and female customers on a monthly basis.

Best Product Line by Customer Type
Analysed which product lines are preferred by different customer types(Member vs. Normal).

Identifying Repeat Customers
Identified customers who made repeat purchases within a specific time frame (e.g., within 30
days).

Finding Top 5 Customers by Sales Volume
Shown top 5 customers who have generated the most sales Revenue.

Analyzing Sales Trends by Day of the Week
analyzed the sales patterns to determine which day of the week
brings the highest sales.
